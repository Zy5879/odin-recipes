Maple Salmon

Description

This is the best and most delicious salmon recipe. Maple is great on everything, people who usually don't enjoy Salmon enjoy the rich taste the the maple adds with the Salmon dish!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound salmon

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, mix the maple syrup, soy sauce, garlic, garlic salt, and pepper.
  2. Place salmon in a shallow glass baking dish, and coat with the maple syrup mixture. Cover the dish, and marinate salmon in the refrigerator for 30 mintues, turning once
  3. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C.)
  4. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven, and bake salmon uncovered for 20 minutes, or until flaked with a fork.
