French Toast

Description

There are many fancy variations of this French Toast. this recipe works with many types of breads, white, whole wheat, cinnamon raisin, italian, or french! Serve it hot with butter and maple syrup.

Ingredients

  • 6 thick slices of bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 2/3 cup of milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspooon ground nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Beat together egg, milk, salt, desired spice and vanilla
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or skillet over medium-high heat
  3. Dunk each slice of bread in egg mixture, soaking both sides. Place in pan, and cook on both sides untill golden. Serve hot
